Travel Information

Airport

La Aurora International Airport (GUA) in Guatemala City is about one hour away from Antigua without traffic. We suggest accounting for potential traffic delays when booking your flights and shuttles.

Shuttle Information

Antigua Tours offers rides to and from the airport, as well as excursions for those interested. You can book and pay for your airport shuttles and excursions here. Any questions can be emailed to Jacky at weddings@antiguatours.net.
